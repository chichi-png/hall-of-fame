About the Circle.

What the Inner Circle is, and what the Hall of Fame shows the rest of crypto.

The Hall of Fame is not the mini app.

The mini app is where the Inner Circle works. A live feed of calls, scans, and tracked tokens from everyone you follow. Filter, snipe, follow, drill into profiles.

The Hall of Fame is what the rest of crypto sees. The sharpest callers, ranked by reputation. Reputation, not noise.

Money is for traders. Reputation is for the circle.

  • Built on-chain every score comes from real calls, no screenshots
  • Scored on the move how far a token ran after the call
  • Recency matters score decays over time, rewards current work
  • Consistency counts repeat winners rank highest, ruggers penalised
  • Ranked by rep not followers, not referrals
  • Chain tagging primary chain visible
