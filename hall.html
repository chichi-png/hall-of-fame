// 03 / about the inner circle

The Hall of Fame is not the mini app.

The mini app is where the Inner Circle works. A live feed of calls, scans, and tracked tokens from everyone you follow. Filter, snipe, follow, drill into profiles.

The Hall of Fame is what the rest of crypto sees. The top of the cohort, frozen weekly. Status, not actions.

Membership is decided upstream. The verified badge is reserved for A+. Money is for traders. Status is for the cohort.

  • Verified on-chain stats sourced from PostHog
  • Weekly refresh numbers update every Monday
  • Dynamic score decays over time, rewards current work
  • All-time stats profile keeps the receipts
  • Verified badge A+ only, mirrored on X
  • Chain tagging primary expertise visible
